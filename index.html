religious dietary restrictions

We would communicate with people who have a variety of backgrounds while studying abroad. Therefore, we must know the foods which people who believe in a religion can eat. That will probably be helpful to you when you go abroad. Let's check it! Please move the cursor over the icons below.

Islam

pork, alcohol, blood and meat that has not been properly treated religiously

Buddhism

Meat in general (beef), chives (go-kun: garlic, garlic, scallions, onions, chives)

Christianity

Meat in general, alcohol, coffee, tea and tobacco

Hinduism

Cows, pork, seafood in general, eggs, raw foods, chives (garlic, garlic, scallions, onions, chives)

Judaism

Pig, blood, some seafoods, meat that has not been properly treated religiously and conbination of dairy and meat.

Vegetarian

They never claim a life or eat the food you get hurt.
